week three

Experience continuing the development of my website: When it comes to the process of developing my website, it has been quite slow as it took me a while to establish the wireframe of my website such as what I want to portray in my website, where I want to portray it and how. However, within this week I have managed to finish it and I am quite happy with the results of it, and I cannot wait for it to look like my basic wireframe. The reason I took so long when it came to developing my wireframe was that I wanted my website to have all these extravagant features but then I grew to dislike them and realised that I would want to create something minimal. When it comes to the coding part of my website, I have only done the basics in HTML and experimenting with some CSS but nothing much for now, I hope in the next few weeks to come that I can do more coding for my website in both HTML and CSS.

Reflection on readings and lecture: After attending the lecture and engaging with the reading ‘Conventions and Context – by Mailloux’. The lecture tied into what UI/UX is by touching on the keyword ‘interactivity’ and what it is, for UX can be defined as your interaction as the user with the product and how interacting with the product makes you feel. It is important to have great UI/UX for it plays an important role in how the end user engages with it ensuring that when they engage with it, it is easy for them to grasp the basics of the website by using appropriate conventions– linking to the idea of establishing a user-friendly interface/website.

Thoughts on Supplementary material: I find the supplementary material to be quite informative and useful, for if I need something or if there is something I am unsure of, I can refer and take my time going through these lectures before seeking further help as the answer to the help I might need might be in the video. Watching them has been very insightful as I feel that I am gaining a better understanding of the work and it provides that extra information to the lectures done for the week.
